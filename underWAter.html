DESCRIPTION
This is the poster I design for KOI Thé, it is a bubble tea shop. This is the poster I designed with the UN SDG 17 Goals that I chose, life below water. I would like to promote KOI Thé milk tea with a theme about life below water. I chose this theme because I saw the issue of ocean pollution becoming more serious now. I hope people will think about the sea when they see this poster.My slogan is Save the ocean with BB bear

My tones of voice are protective, caring, and happy. The colours that I selected are more oceanic, such as blue and greenish blue. I use more cool colours to show the ocean scene. The main illustration I want to let people see is BB bear and KOI Thé's logo, so to avoid other illustrations attracting attention, I designed the marine life illustration in blue, the same colour as the background, but I also added some warm colours to the marine life illustration, such as orange and pink, to prevent them from looking like the background. For example,the sea turtle used greenish blue and pink.

The illustration that I designed is more cute and cartoon-style. I use the gradient tool in almost all of my illustrations because it looks dreamy and can show the depth of an object.I also adjusted the opacity of the illustration to make the poster look more layered. I also use the same stroke in my design to let all things unite.

My design is a BB bear sitting inside a submarine, and I designed the submarine in KOI Thé's cup. BB bear is exploring the ocean. The marine life I choose is marine life in Malaysia, such as whales and sea turtles. Sea turtles and dugongs are known as the marine life affected by ocean pollution in Malaysia.
